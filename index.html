SPLC Hate Map URLs

The Southern Poverty Law Center have done brave and extensive work documenting known hate groups and extremists as part of their Extremist Files and Hate Map websites. We are trying to identify the social media accounts (Twitter and Facebook) and website URLs for these organizations to help study the impact that these groups are having on the Web.

If you have a moment please help us by searching for an organization or three on Twitter, Facebook and in Google and enter in the authoritative URLs if you can find them. Remember, not all of these organizations will have social media accounts or even a website.

The spreadsheet was derived from the geo-json that is behind the Hate Map. If you have any questions or comments please user our GitHub issue tracker.
